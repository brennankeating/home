NJ Ghost Burger

I helped NJ Ghost Burger revamp their website and social media presence to match the excitement of their legendary burgers on wheels.

Overview

I was stoked to team up with NJ Ghost Burger, the award-winning food truck serving up out-of-this-world eats on wheels. Our mission was to give their online presence a major overhaul, redesigning their website and amplifying their social media marketing efforts to match the excitement of their signature burgers and spooky charm.

The Work

Results

As I worked with NJ Ghost Burger, we saw significant improvements across their website and social media presence. The newly designed website features a modern, mobile-friendly design that showcases the food truck's unique brand and menu offerings. Meanwhile, targeted campaigns and engaging content helped increase their online visibility and appeal to their target audience.
