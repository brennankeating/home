Typography Study

An exploration of typographic composition and hierarchy, investigating how letterforms can be arranged to create visual impact and communicate beyond their literal meaning. This study examines the interplay between type, space, and structure.

Overview

The Typography project has influenced my approach to type-driven design across a wide range of client work. Insights gained from exploring multiple fonts and typographic systems have been applied to editorial layouts, brand identities, and digital interfaces, resulting in more refined, cohesive, and impactful typographic solutions.

Results
