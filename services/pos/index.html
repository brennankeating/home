Common Questions

POS, Answered

Which POS system is right for my business?

It depends on your industry, transaction volume, hardware needs, and the software you already use. The comparison above is a starting point. For a recommendation tailored to your business, get in touch and I'll narrow it down for you.

Can you set up and integrate the system for me?

Yes. I handle the full setup: hardware, payment configuration, and integrations with tools like QuickBooks, e-commerce platforms, and online ordering. Your POS works end to end from day one.

Are you tied to a specific POS or payment processor?

No. I'm independent, so I recommend whatever genuinely fits your business instead of pushing one brand, including options that let you offset card fees through surcharging or dual pricing.

How much does a POS system cost?

There are two parts: monthly software (anywhere from free to a few hundred dollars) and processing fees, which depend on your pricing model: surcharge, dual pricing, or interchange. Because it varies with your volume and setup, reach out for an exact quote.

Can you help lower my processing fees?

Often, yes. Many of the platforms above support surcharging or dual pricing, which shift some or all of the card fees off your bottom line. Send me your current statement and I'll show you where you can save.
